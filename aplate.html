A PLATE

Project Summary

A plate is startup company aiming to expand everyone’s networking in a social environment via its own social platform. The goal of the ‘aplate’ company is offer people to meet new people around a table which is specified to be given some discount from registered restaurant

Project Vision

Due to the disease that is currently spreading around the world, people are confined to their homes, and because of this situation, everyone’s relationship with technology has started to develop even more. Some took their classes from home, some took care of their work from home, but in most cases, they had to do it alone and were removed from social life. Human beings are social creatures and become happy by socializing.

Project / Business Requirements

A plate is startup company aiming to expand everyone’s networking in a social environment via its own social platform. The goal of the ‘aplate’ company is offer people to meet new people around a table which is specified to be given some discount from registered restaurant

Project Plan

A plate will offer a unique platform to everyone for expanding network, give the opportunity to the restaurant to create a campaign and boost the sales. Our primary strategy is to meet a tentatively identified need for integrated solutions in the field of collaborative learning environments by running a direct marketing campaign alongside social media to increase effectiveness.
